"Polylog" Consulting Group: we create and support the positive attitude of our society towards organizations, ideologies and socio-political figures. Using the professional slang, our job is called marketing communications management, public relations, promotion – and in more broad terms it means the analysis of minds, the creation and dissemination of the news that help to solve our clients’ problems. For each and every one of them, we find an effective, personal, well-researched decision. And we can do it for you.

Services

  1. Comprehensive PR support for clients' products and brands, including relevant media-relations.
  2. GR and lobbying issues for business representatives.
  3. Development, production of corporate media: newspapers, magazines, brochures, customer journals etc.
  4. Event marketing and BTL-campaigns.
  5. PR-support for clients' marketing activities/initiatives.
  6. Analytics & research / Reputation crisis management.
  7. Corporate/personal educational programs: media-relations, external communications, personal performance skills, etc.
